Ingredients:
Rajma (Red Kidney Beans) 1 Cup
Water 5 Cups
Salt & Pepper to taste
Haldi 0.25 tsp
Kasuri Methi - 2 tsps
Chili Powder - 1 tsp
Oil - 2 tsps

Soak Rajma for 5 hrs and then keep it for boil. Let it boil for 30 mins. Add salt to Rajma. In a seperate pan, add oil to heat and add Haldi and Kasuri Methi and all spices. Mix the spices in Rajma and enjoy.
